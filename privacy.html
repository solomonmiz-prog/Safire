Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

Safire Vintage values your privacy. This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

  • Contact details you provide at checkout, including name, email, phone, and shipping address.
  • Order details such as products purchased, totals, and order timing.
  • Technical data like browser type and pages visited for site performance and analytics.

How We Use Information

  • To process and fulfill orders.
  • To send transactional communications such as order confirmations and support messages.
  • To improve site performance, shopping flow, and product experience.

Payments

Payments are processed through Stripe. Safire Vintage does not store full card numbers in this site.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Information is shared only with service providers needed to run the store, such as payment and shipping services.

Data Retention

We retain order and customer details only as long as needed for business operations, legal obligations, and support.

Your Rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us.

Contact

For privacy requests, contact safireorders@gmail.com.
