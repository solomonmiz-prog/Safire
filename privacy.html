Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

SAFIRE values your trust. This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Order details: name, email, phone number, shipping address, and purchase information.
  • Transaction details: payment status and checkout metadata from Stripe.
  • Technical information: browser/device basics, pages viewed, and performance diagnostics.

2. How We Use Information

  • To process orders, payments, and shipping.
  • To provide customer support and order updates.
  • To improve website reliability, speed, and user experience.
  • To prevent abuse, fraud, or unauthorized activity.

3. Payments and Security

Card payments are processed securely through Stripe. SAFIRE does not store full payment card numbers on this website.

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect personal information.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with services needed to operate the store, such as payment processors, shipping carriers, and technical infrastructure providers.

5. Data Retention

We retain order and support records for as long as needed for operations, legal compliance, and dispute resolution, then delete or anonymize data where appropriate.

6. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of personal information we hold about you, subject to legal requirements.

You may also request that we limit or stop certain processing where applicable.

7. Cookies and Similar Tools

We may use browser storage or similar tools for cart functionality, session behavior, and essential site performance. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

8. Policy Updates

We may revise this Privacy Policy as the store evolves. The updated date at the top reflects the latest version.

9. Contact

For privacy requests, email safireorders@gmail.com.
